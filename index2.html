Targeting Fraud, Waste and Abuse in healthcare insurance

Using Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, Kirontech's Health Insurance Platform helps customers become more productive, efficient and profitable.

ClaimsAI

ClaimsAi - the Curacel AI solution - is a state-of-the-art Machine-Learning and AI software platform that detects FWA and errors in customer data, on premises or in the cloud.

ClaimScope

Claim Management Platform

When claims data enters the platform, each claim passes through an extensive data validation process powered by ClaimScope engine, producing risk scoring metrics and pinpointing FWA in each claim.

ClaimScope comprises simple rules like age and gender as well as extensive machine learning algorithms that consider the full treatment histories.

CaseHound

Clear and Efficient FWA Investigation

Once individual claims are validated and sanitized, CaseHound sniffs its way through the entire database looking for behavioural patterns, collusion networks, and inefficiencies such as upcoding. 

The unsupervised graph based sweeps learn and adapt to new relevant patterns, spotting complicated patterns that are not visible to unaided human eye.

KironBI

Business INTELLIGENCE Platform

For top level managers, having the right tools to overview and monitor the business is paramount.  KironBI allows you to summarise and quickly produce management-level reports of your data.

With KironBI, you can narrow down on metrics and statistics such as processed claims, most common violations, total riskiness and estimated FWA figures.

METHODOLOGY

The methology underpinning Kirontech Health Insurance Platform is varied and game-changing. Our NLP solution helps customers with coding and incomplete data, whilst Kironmed provides the medical reference data for enrichment.  The heart of the platform is our Unsupervised Graph Based Learning engine that highlights the essential analytical and behavioural patterns, thus giving the customer's investigative teams a leg up over the competition.

UGL

Dynamic unsupervised graph-based learning

Unsupervised learning identifies recurrent but previously unknown patterns in your data. The algorithm searches for abnormal associations without being told what to look for by a human being.

It can learn and adapt to new relevant patterns much faster than a human being and spot complicated relationships that are not visible to unaided eye.

KironMed

Semantic Networks

Kirontech medical semantic network (KironMed) collects data from more than 60 medical sources, combining it to form one of the most comprehensive reference networks in existence.

Our claim handling algorithms leverage these networks to help them learn and to ensure that our customers' claims data is enriched to the max.

Medical NLP

Natural Language Processing

Using a combination of artificial intelligence and information engineering, Kirontech's coding engine allows us to process claims data even with incomplete or missing diagnosis, service or drug codes.

By using advanced NLP - based techniques, the coding engine can accurately suggest codes for conditions and services as long as descriptions are provided.

API

The Developer API allows for system-level integration as part of the live claim validation process.  As customer submits claims to the Platform via the REST API, live feedback is provided with rule alerts, line item level details and risk scoring.  The information is templated and standardised, making for easy integration with in-house systems and processes.

ClaimScope API

LIVE CLAIM VALIDATION

ClaimScope API provides the main integration point for the customer.  The customer can leverage the full power of the Kirontech Platform using simple REST/json calls.

With simple and scalable hardware configurations, the software can handle both realtime and bulk loads depending on the use case.

Live Alerts

SPOT FWA IN REAL TIME

Prevention is better than cure.  As claims are submitted in real time, the Platform responds with live alerts. The alerts can easily be integrated into existing workflows to ensure you capture inefficiencies even before they arise.

The types of alerts range from simple age/gender based rules to complex AI driven pattern searches.

Security

MILITARY-GRADE SECURITY TO PROTECT YOUR DATA

To protect customer data, the Platform can be deployed on premises or VPN in secure cloud. All certificates are supplied by the customer, and the customer retains full control of user access management and application tokens.

We work with your data in your infrastructure - your data never leaves your control.
